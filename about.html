About Burdwan South Division of WBSEDCL

WBSEDCL stands for West Bengal State Electricity Distribution Company Limited. It is a government-owned utility company responsible for distributing electricity to consumers in the state of West Bengal, India. WBSEDCL was formed after the restructuring of the erstwhile West Bengal State Electricity Board in 2007. The company has a customer base of over 1.86 crore and serves an area of approximately 87,000 sq. km. WBSEDCL is responsible for the distribution of power to all categories of consumers including domestic, commercial, industrial, and agricultural consumers. It is also responsible for the maintenance and upgradation of the distribution infrastructure in the state.
